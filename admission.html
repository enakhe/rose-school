Instead, we carefully review each applicant’s academic and personal background to find those who will be a great fit for our diverse community. Join us and become part of a vibrant environment dedicated to making a positive impact on the world.

Our school values the unique perspectives and backgrounds each student brings, fostering an inclusive environment where everyone can thrive. By joining us, you will become part of a dynamic and forward-thinking institution committed to making a positive difference in the world.

The Application Process

1

Start Online Submission

Begin your application process by visiting our admissions portal and creating an account. Fill out the online application form with accurate and complete information.

2

Submit The Form

Once you have completed the form, review all entries to ensure accuracy. Submit the form electronically through our admissions portal.

3

Review The Submission

Our admissions team will review your submitted application to ensure all required information and initial qualifications are met.

4

Gather Necessary Documents

After your application has been reviewed, gather all required documents such as academic transcripts, recommendation letters, identification, and any other specified materials. Passport photographs, Birth certificate (photocopy), Academic records of previous school

5

Interviewing Process

Qualified applicants will be invited for an interview. This may be conducted in person or online, providing an opportunity to discuss your application and answer any questions.

6

Last Decision

After the interview and review of all documents, our admissions committee will make the final decision. You will be notified of the outcome via email or through our admissions portal.

Things To Know First

Ensure you have a strong academic record and participate in extracurricular activities. Familiarize yourself with our school’s values and admission criteria.

You will need :

Provide official transcripts from previous schools attended. These should include records of grades and any standardized test scores.

Fill out the online application form with accurate and complete information. Ensure all required fields are properly filled out before submission.

Submit letters of recommendation from teachers, counselors, or other professionals who can speak to your academic abilities and character.
