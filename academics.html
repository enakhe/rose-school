Dedicated to Excellence

Rose International School is committed to offering all our students a broad and balanced curriculum enriched with rewarding and stimulating activities. This approach prepares them for a fulfilling social and cultural life.

Whether through our books or hands-on training, we ensure each student receives personal attention to excel in every subject, achieve better scores, and secure a brighter future.

Curriculum Overview

Rose International School is dedicated to offering our students a comprehensive and balanced curriculum, enriched with rewarding and stimulating activities, to prepare them for an enriched social and cultural life.

Our curriculum is meticulously designed to cover a broad spectrum of subjects, ensuring that students receive a well-rounded education. From the core subjects of mathematics, science, and language arts to humanities, social sciences, and technology, we provide a robust academic foundation. Our aim is to cultivate critical thinking, creativity, and a love for lifelong learning.

Computer Science

Our life goals are often shaped by our understanding of the world. Many children begin learning to code with the aspiration of creating games. We expand their horizons by demonstrating how studying computer science can open doors to careers in filmmaking and beyond.

Fine Art

The art and design curriculum is designed to ensure that all students achieve the following objectives: produce creative work that explores their ideas and documents their experiences, and become proficient in various techniques including drawing, painting, sculpture, and other forms of art, craft, and design.

Basic Technology

Basic Technology program is designed to equip students with essential technological skills and knowledge that are crucial in today's rapidly advancing world. This program lays the foundation for understanding and utilizing technology effectively, preparing students for advanced studies applications in various fields.

Science

The curriculum emphasizes the importance of beginning with children’s own ideas and learning through interactions with objects, materials, and their classmates. This approach fosters creativity, critical thinking, and collaboration, allowing children to explore and understand the world around them.

Mathematics

Mathematics can be understood as the science of magnitude, number, shape, space, and their relationships. It also serves as a universal language, expressed through symbols and diagrams. This discipline allows us to model and solve real-world problems, understand patterns, and make predictions.

Library

The library is the heart of our academic community. It is a vibrant, welcoming space where students can immerse themselves in the world of books, research, and learning. Our library offers a vast collection of resources, modern facilities, and a quiet environment conducive to study and exploration.
